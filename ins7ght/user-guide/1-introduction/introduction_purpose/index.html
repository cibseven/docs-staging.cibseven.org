Purpose

Purpose of CIB ins7ght

The purpose of CIB ins7ght is to transform raw process execution data into actionable intelligence.
It enables organizations to understand how their automated workflows perform, identify where time and resources are being lost, and uncover opportunities for optimization and cost savings.

CIB seven, orchestrates workflows and stores detailed process execution data. That data remains not analyzed. CIB ins7ght leverages this data to create meaningful insights and allows users to visualize, measure, and continuously improve their processes with confidence.

The Problems It Solves

In many organizations, process data exists but is underutilized.
Teams struggle to answer questions like:

  • How long do our processes actually take from start to finish?
  • Where are the bottlenecks causing delays?
  • How much of our process time is spent on manual tasks?
  • Are we truly achieving the efficiency promised by automation?

Without the right tools, identifying these insights can require complex queries, manual reporting, or even guesswork.
CIB ins7ght eliminates these barriers by providing an intuitive interface and built-in analytics that automatically reveal key performance patterns.

How It Supports Business Process Optimization

CIB ins7ght serves as a foundation for data-driven process improvement. It allows organizations to:

  • Monitor performance continuously: visualize how processes behave in real-time and detect inefficiencies early.
  • Identify and remove bottlenecks: analyze which activities consume the most time or resources.
  • Support strategic decisions: empower managers and analysts with knowledge to prioritize where to improve next.

By integrating with existing CIB seven data and turning it into clear, visual insights, CIB ins7ght bridges the gap between technical process execution and business-level understanding, helping organizations continuously optimize their operations.
